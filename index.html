About

A life dedicated to business excellence and social impact

Jagannath Naidu Lenka is a distinguished business leader serving as the CEO of Garnet Energy. His journey from academic excellence at Tulane University to the helm of a successful energy company exemplifies his drive for achievement and innovation.

Beyond the boardroom, Jagannath is deeply committed to education. His passion for teaching stems from a belief that knowledge is the greatest gift one can share. Whether mentoring young professionals or supporting educational initiatives, he consistently invests in the future generation.

As a philanthropist, he channels his success into meaningful causes, believing that true prosperity lies in lifting others along the way.

Philosophy

Success is not just about what you accomplish in your own life, but about what you inspire others to achieve. Education is the foundation upon which we build better futures — for ourselves and for generations to come.
